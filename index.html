Life of Nar-Nar

Nar-nar was born on a farm in Halifax in 2009. A few weeks later she met her first servant who she lived with in Halifax for several years.

In 2012 she moved and became a Toronto resident where she lived as an indoor cat. During this time, she moved frequently and lived in over 7 different homes. This is when she met her second servant.

Throughout her life, she has been considered adorable to her detriment, as this rallied flocks of amateur photographers during house parties who would surround her and take photos. She often had no choice but to shed blood.

She is currently living a life fit for royalty as her two servants cater to her every need.
